How Liveness Check Works
Liveness detection checks if you're a real person — not a photo, video replay, or AI deepfake.
1
Position your face in the camera frame, centered and well-lit.
Stay 30–60cm from the screen
2
Click "Run Liveness Check" and follow on-screen prompts.
You may be asked to blink or turn your head
3
Stay still during the scan (about 5 seconds).
Avoid harsh backlighting
4
View your result — score, liveness verdict, and explanation.
A certificate is issued for PASS results
